How Interview Coach works

Practice like it's real. Walk in like you've done it before.

1

Meteor analyzes the job

Paste the job description. Meteor identifies the exact skills, seniority signals, and question types the company typically uses — tailoring your prep to that specific role.

2

Practice with AI questions

Work through behavioral, technical, and system design questions generated for your exact role. Answer in your own words, at your own pace — just like a real interview.

3

Get feedback and improve

After each answer, Meteor scores your response, highlights what worked, and suggests exactly how to make it stronger. Track improvement across sessions.
