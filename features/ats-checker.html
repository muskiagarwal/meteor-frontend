How ATS Checker works

A precise score and an exact fix list — in minutes.

1

Upload your resume

Drop your PDF or Word document. Meteor parses every section — experience, skills, education, summary — and flags any formatting issues that trip up ATS systems.

2

Add the job description

Paste the full job description. Meteor extracts required skills, preferred qualifications, and company-specific language to benchmark your resume against.

3

Get your score and fix list

Receive an exact 0–100 ATS score, a keyword gap analysis, formatting warnings, and one-click fixes. See exactly what's holding you back — and fix it instantly.
