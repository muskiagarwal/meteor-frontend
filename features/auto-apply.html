How Auto-Apply works

Three steps to put your job search on autopilot.

1

Set your preferences

Tell Meteor what you're looking for — role, location, salary range, remote preference, and companies to avoid. Takes under 3 minutes.

2

AI matches & tailors

Meteor scans thousands of job boards daily, finds your best matches, and personalizes your resume and cover letter for each role using AI.

3

Applications sent automatically

Meteor submits applications across all major ATS platforms — Greenhouse, Lever, Workday — and updates your tracker in real time.
