How Resume Builder works

From upload to tailored resume in three simple steps.

1

Upload your base resume

Drop in your existing resume or start from scratch with one of Meteor's professionally designed templates. We parse everything automatically.

2

Paste a job description

Copy the job description from any job board. Meteor extracts the key requirements, preferred skills, and language the company uses.

3

Meteor tailors and optimizes

AI rewrites your bullet points, injects relevant keywords, reorders your sections for impact, and exports a clean, ATS-safe PDF — ready to send.
