How Job Tracker works

Your entire job search pipeline, organized automatically.

1

Applications logged automatically

Every job Meteor applies to is instantly added to your board. No manual entry. Each card includes the role, company, date applied, and a direct link to the listing.

2

Move cards through stages

Drag cards from Applied → Screening → Interview → Offer as your search progresses. Add notes, contacts, and deadlines to each card so nothing slips through the cracks.

3

Smart follow-up reminders

Meteor alerts you when it's been 5+ days without a response and suggests the perfect follow-up message. Follow up more, hear back more.
